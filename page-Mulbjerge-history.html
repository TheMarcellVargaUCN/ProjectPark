Mulbjerge

Mulbjerge and the formation of Lille Vildmose 6000 years ago in the Stone Age, Lille Vildmose was covered by sea, and Mulbjerge lay like an island in the flat landscape at a distance of 5 km from the rest of Himmerland. When northern Denmark began to rise, Mulbjerge became - landlocked with the rest of Himmerland. In the low, swampy area those between Mulbjerge and Smidie the bog gradually formed. Off the coast, reefs are now forming and the shallow areas are in day very valuable for bird life.
