Bjergbanestien

According to the 1933 roadmap the railway had only one track, however, two trains could be in each direction pass each other at stations with detours. Between the stations there was also stops - called step boards - where people who wanted to take the train even turn on a signal to show the driver, that new travelers should join. On the stretch Godthåb - Nibe there were 5 step boards. The roller coaster today Today, all step boards and most station buildings removed. However, the station building for Sønderholm exists Station still, but it is now converted into private residence -without public access.
