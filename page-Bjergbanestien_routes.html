ROUTES NATURE HISTORY

Bjergbanestien

Bjergbanestien, local cycle route 700, is an approx. 14 km long cycle and walking path from Svenstrup via Oldstien to Godthåb and Nibe.

From Svenstrup there is a path connection to the recreational areas in Østerådalen and further towards Aalborg.

From Skalskoven west of Nibe, the path continues along Nørregade, which leads to the center of Nibe, where Nibe train station once lay. The railway path continues through Nibe and on towards Hvalpsund along the regional cycle route.
