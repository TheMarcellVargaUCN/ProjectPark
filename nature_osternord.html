Østerådalen Nord

The varied nature of Østerådalen forms the basis for a rich wildlife. The wetlands attract several different bird species - both breeding and migratory birds. Many swans and geese arrive here from northern Scandinavia, Russia and the Arctic as winter visitors.
Only 3 km from Aalborg Centrum you will find Østerådalen North - and approx. 100 ha large, protected nature area that is owned by Aalborg Municipality.
In Østerådalen there are many different plant communities eg reed forest, elle bush, forest, wet and dry meadows, each with their quite distinctive species associated.
