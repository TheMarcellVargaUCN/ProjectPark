Østerådalen Syd

In the late 1800s, the creek became, like so many others places in Denmark, straightened out. The moist areas were drained and converted to farmland. Today, agriculture is practiced in most of Østerådalen South. To the northwest and southeast, some relatively large land farms are seen, operated in the traditional way with field crops or dairy cattle. In the central and southwestern part of the area is seen a number of extensively run leisure farms primarily with grazing and hay.
