Østerådalen Syd

In Hals Mose you can follow three local walking routes that stretch from Hals town in the south to the old Hals Mose in the north. The routes are 5, 10 and 12.5 km. The routes consist of both paths, field, forest and asphalt roads, but are most suitable for pedestrian traffic.
